About Us

Nine By Nine Minecraft, originally a DireWolf20 Server, was founded on the principles of: courtesy, respect, and friendship. We (The Executive Board) didn't like all the "Pay To Play" Minecraft servers out there, so we set out to be something different, something better, and thus Nine By Nine was born.
