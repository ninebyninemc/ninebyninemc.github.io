About Us

TenEight Roleplay Community is a FiveM Server that was started by a few people who wanted serious roleplay. We were tired of all the servers where nobody would do serious roleplay, so we started our own. Running on powerful hardware 7th Generation Intel hardware and with a gigabit ethernet connection, we aim for smooth gameplay in a world full of laggy servers. So, if you want fun and serious roleplay check us out, and join today. We are a diverse community, we have people from all walks of life. A few of our major rules of thumb are; Don't say something to someone that you wouldn't say to their face (No Cyber-Thuggin'), and Be respectful to each other regardless of race, creed, color, or gender. There is no appeals process for bans involving derogatory terminology. If you see a pig, a sheep and a cow walk out of the bank, don’t shoot them, call their owner (We Mean You, Jim). Sorry if this paragraph got a bit random, but some things just had to be said...
