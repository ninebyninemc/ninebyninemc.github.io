About Us

Nine By Nine Roleplay Community is a FiveM Server that was started by a few people who wanted serious roleplay. We were tired of all the servers where nobody would do serious roleplay, so we started our own. Running on powerful hardware and a gigabit ethernet connection, we aim for smooth gameplay in a world full of laggy servers. So, if you want fun and serious roleplay check us out, and join today. We are a diverse community, we have people from all walks of life (Powpow is an apache attack helicopter, Joe is a PL-01 tank). Don't say something to someone that you wouldn't say to their face (No Cyber-Thuggin'). We expect people to be respectful to each other regardless of race, creed, color, or gender.
