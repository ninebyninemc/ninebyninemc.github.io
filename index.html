Hello Everyone,

We regret to inform you that Nine By Nine is hereby semi-permanently closed. Unfortunately, The Executive Board has too much going on in-real-life to dedicate the necessary time to running Nine By Nine’s FiveM server. We enjoyed the time that we were open and hope everyone else did too. As of today (8/31/17) we will no longer be processing any applications. Our website will be reduced to this single page, and our TeamSpeak 3 server will be turned off.

We want to thank everyone for the wonderful times we had together and we hope that we are afforded the opportunity to re-open at some point in the future.

Sincerely:

Nine By Nine Executive Board
