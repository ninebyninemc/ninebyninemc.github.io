Donation Info

To start off we must state, "We are NOT a Pay-To-Play type server!", which means your donations will not get you some extreme advantage over your fellow minecraftians, such as but not limited to; Flight, Creative Mode, Banned Items, Extremely Rare Items, and Super Enchanted Items. Now, with this in mind, we do encourage you to donate, we are a non-profit server, meaning that the staff and owner get no money from the server, all money that you give us we put right back into running the server, paying for hosting, and anything else we need to do to better the quality of your gameplay. So please, help us keep the lights on, and donate, even if it is only $5 or $10.
